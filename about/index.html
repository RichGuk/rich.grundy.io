Born and raised in Birmingham, UK. I've been building web applications for over ten years. I love all things open source and code wise Ruby is my language of choice, vim my editor.

I have a degree in Computer Science from Aberystwyth university. I worked as a PHP developer before becoming a Ruby on Rails developer. I am currently IT manager for one of the UK's online printer ink retailers, Stinkyink.com.

When I'm not hacking on code I will most likely be out shredding mountain bike trials with my fantastic mates, or out enjoying a meal. I'm also known to enjoy computer games from time-to-time.

You can contact me via Twitter (or email) and can view my code / projects on Github.
